Welcome to Māori Culture

Māori culture is deeply rooted in connection — to the land, ancestors, community, and spirit. Central to this culture is the concept of whanaungatanga, which emphasises relationships, kinship, and belonging. Other key values include manaakitanga (hospitality) and kaitiakitanga (guardianship of the environment). These practices are passed down through storytelling, art, performance, and language.

Cultural Elements of Māori

Element Description
HarakekeFlax weaving used in traditional clothing and baskets
